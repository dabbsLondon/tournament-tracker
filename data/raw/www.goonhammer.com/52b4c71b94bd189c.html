        Thoughts<\/h3>\n

        Lol, an excellent showdown this week that pits two armies that can BOTH charge and countercharge the foe to death in the opposing turn. The Wrath build is very interesting – it avoids going in too hard on melee, instead leveraging JUST enough counter-charge to support a whole host of bikers and speeders. These have been creeping up in use in recent months, and pack excellent output into the many other marine equivalent armies we are seeing, especially the pressure ones. Guard those guns with a front line of the Lion and some DWK, and we are cooking.<\/p>\n

        The Spite list is well established now – a swingy fire base of Scourges, then plenty of Wyches and Hellions. The latter really don’t like Deathwing Knights, who are an ideal profile to absorb them once\u00a0Armour of Contempt\u00a0<\/strong>is applied. That, and the rapid death of any Wych Cults units that stray into the Dark Angel firing lanes, keeps this suitably close. I have to say i’d have backed the Dark Kin here but it looks like with careful positioning and patience, the Sons of the Lion snuck the narrowest of wins. Fantastic work to you both! Nothing like a one point final. Except for that time Aarron’s Caladius killed all my Acolytes in melee. That one still stings.<\/p>\n
