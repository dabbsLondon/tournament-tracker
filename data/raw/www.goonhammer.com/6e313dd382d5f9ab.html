          Thoughts<\/h3>\n

          The return of last summer’s meta continues, as a recently resurgent Death Guard find themselves up against an even more recently resurgent Imperial Knights. The Questoris Companions find themselves able to roam free again in the absence of Aeldari to keep them down, and they are ready to rush and stat-check a whole bunch of armies teching for a previously infantry heavy meta.<\/p>\n

          The Mortarion’s Hammer DG packs some cute tricks including the ability to cast Contagion around to get those many, many units of Deathshroud into spots they’re not welcome. This was a really strong strategy in the summer and many of the other tricks here – getting vehicles through walls – are excellent in applying different types of pressure too. However, Hammer never loved big skew, and this is no different. Lots and lots of Armigers rushing forward, with higher OC than the drones, followed by several big Knights is\u00a0 areal issue for the Death Guard, and they were quite simply overwhelmed here! A very one-sided finale.<\/p>\n
